about us

Tracking the vaccination effort, one datapoint at a time

We are a junior development team working at a company called J.O.D.E.A.

Our task is to track the vaccination effort within Missouri.

Our goal is to be able to provide data and statistics involving vaccinations in Missouri to the CDC and health providers, making sure to strictly follow all HIPAA guidelines.
